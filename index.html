About Us

Our Mission

To empower and develop highly skilled EV engineering talent by providing practical, application-based training and advanced laboratory solutions that enable students to gain real industry exposure, hands-on experience, and job-ready competencies essential for the electric mobility revolution.

About the Company

CIATS is a leading training and technology solutions provider dedicated to shaping the future of electric mobility education. We specialize in delivering industry-oriented EV training programs, building advanced laboratory setups, and offering hands-on skill development modules that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering. Our team works closely with educational institutions, industry partners, and emerging EV professionals to ensure that learners acquire practical competencies, technical confidence, and a deep understanding of modern electric vehicle systems. With a strong focus on innovation, safety, and industry relevance, CIATS is committed to empowering the next generation of EV engineers and supporting India's transition to sustainable mobility.

Our Services

Empowering Institutions with Practical EV Education & Infrastructure

EV Workshops & Training

Hands-on practical workshops on 2-Wheeler and 3-Wheeler Electric Vehicles, enabling students to understand EV architecture, components, subsystems, and real-time operational techniques directly from industry experts.

Electric Vehicle Lab Setup

Turnkey EV practical lab installation for engineering colleges with operational EV components, simulation test benches, wiring & diagnostics panels, and experiment modules for real industrial learning.

Technical Support & Assistance

Continuous technical guidance, faculty training, maintenance assistance, and operational support ensuring effective usage of EV labs, tools, and training systems throughout the year.
