About Us

Our Mission

To empower and develop highly skilled EV engineering talent by providing practical, application-based training and advanced laboratory solutions that enable students to gain real industry exposure, hands-on experience, and job-ready competencies essential for the electric mobility revolution.

About the Company

CIATS is a leading training and technology solutions provider dedicated to shaping the future of electric mobility education. We specialize in delivering industry-oriented EV training programs, building advanced laboratory setups, and offering hands-on skill development modules that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering. Our team works closely with educational institutions, industry partners, and emerging EV professionals to ensure that learners acquire practical competencies, technical confidence, and a deep understanding of modern electric vehicle systems. With a strong focus on innovation, safety, and industry relevance, CIATS is committed to empowering the next generation of EV engineers and supporting India's transition to sustainable mobility.

Our Services

Empowering Institutions with Practical EV Education, Industry Software & Infrastructure

EV Workshops & Training

Hands-on practical workshops on 2-Wheeler and 3-Wheeler Electric Vehicles covering EV architecture, components, subsystems, diagnostics, and real-time operational techniques by industry experts.

Electric Vehicle Lab Setup

Complete turnkey EV lab installation for engineering colleges including operational EV models, simulation benches, wiring panels, diagnostics tools, and structured experiment modules.

Technical Support & Assistance

Year-round technical support including faculty training, lab maintenance assistance, curriculum guidance, and operational support for effective utilization of EV labs and tools.

Industry Software Training

Access to industry-used software tools for CAD, simulation, automation, data analysis, and AI, enabling students to gain real-world exposure and job-ready technical skills.

Software Training at CIATS

Industry-oriented software training designed to make students job-ready with practical skills

CAD & Product Design

Hands-on training on AutoCAD, SolidWorks, CATIA, Fusion 360, Inventor, and Rhino for industrial-level 2D drafting, 3D modeling, assemblies, and design validation.

Data Analysis & Automation

Advanced Excel with VBA, automation techniques, and data analysis tools to improve productivity, reporting, and industrial workflow optimization.

Programming & Simulation

Practical training on Python, MATLAB, and simulation tools for EV systems, control logic, data processing, modeling, and real-time problem solving.

Visualization & Rendering

Professional visualization using KeyShot, Blender, and Photoshop for product rendering, animations, presentations, and industrial design showcasing.

AI & Smart Tools

Exposure to AI-powered tools like ChatGPT and automation platforms for technical documentation, analysis, coding assistance, and smart engineering workflows.

AR / VR Technologies

Introduction to AR/VR platforms including Meta and HoloLens for immersive learning, virtual labs, and next-generation industrial training.

Industry Projects

Real-world industry projects integrating EV concepts with CAD, simulation, automation, and data analysis for hands-on learning and portfolio development.

Certification & Skill Mapping

Structured certification programs aligned with industry requirements, helping students map skills to real job roles and career pathways.
